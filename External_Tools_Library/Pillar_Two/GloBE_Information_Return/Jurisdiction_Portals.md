# Jurisdiction Filing Portals for GIR

Links to tax authority portals where GIR and related filings are submitted. Use these to understand portal interfaces and requirements.

## United Kingdom

| Portal | URL | Purpose |
|--------|-----|---------|
| **HMRC Online Services** | https://www.gov.uk/log-in-register-hmrc-online-services | Main HMRC portal |
| **Government Gateway** | https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/pillar-2-multinational-top-up-tax | Pillar Two guidance |
| **MTT/DTT Registration** | Via HMRC Online Services | Register for Multinational Top-up Tax |

### UK-Specific Notes
- GIR filed via HMRC digital service
- Separate registration required for MTT
- UK Domestic Top-up Tax (DTT) filed separately

## Practice Tips

1. **Create test accounts** where sandbox environments are available
2. **Review user guides** before attempting filings
3. **Note deadline differences** between jurisdictions
4. **Check for updates** as portals evolve

## Important Cautions

- Portal URLs and interfaces change frequently
- Always verify current portal location with official tax authority websites
- Some portals require pre-registration well before filing deadlines
- Test submissions (where available) may not replicate all production features
