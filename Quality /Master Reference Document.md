| Element Type | Standard Pattern | Examples from Part 1 | Notes |
|--------------|------------------|----------------------|-------|
| Currency (prose) | €X million | "€750 million" | Spell out "million" in prose |

| Currency (OECD quotes) | EUR X million | "EUR 750 million" | Preserve OECD terminology in quotes |
| Percentages | X% or X.X% | "20-50%", "80%" | Whole numbers for ownership |
