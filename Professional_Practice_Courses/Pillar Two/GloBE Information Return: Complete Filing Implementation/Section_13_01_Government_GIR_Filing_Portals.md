# Section 13.1: Government GIR Filing Portals

## PART 4: ESSENTIAL RESOURCES

### Official Resource Directory

---

## Introduction

This section provides a comprehensive directory of government filing portals for GloBE Information Return (GIR) submissions across 25+ jurisdictions. For each jurisdiction, you will find:

- **Portal name and direct URL**
- **Registration requirements**
- **Technical specifications**
- **Help desk contact information**
- **User guide links**

> **Critical Note:** Portal availability and specifications are subject to change. Many jurisdictions are still finalising their GIR filing infrastructure ahead of the first filing deadline of **30 June 2026** (for December 2024 year-ends). Verify current portal status with relevant tax authorities before filing.

---
