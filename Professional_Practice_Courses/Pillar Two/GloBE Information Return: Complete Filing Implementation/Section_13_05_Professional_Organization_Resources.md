# Section 13.5: Professional Organization Resources

## Overview

This section provides a comprehensive directory of professional organization resources for GloBE Information Return (GIR) implementation. These resources complement official government and OECD guidance with practical insights, training opportunities, advocacy updates, and technical analysis from leading tax professional bodies, advisory firms, and industry associations.

**Resource Categories Covered:**
- Big 4 accounting firm resources (free and subscription)
- Global tax professional associations
- National chartered institutes and CPA bodies
- Tax research and analysis services
- Mid-tier firm resources and implementation trackers
- Training and professional development programmes

## Summary

Professional organization resources provide essential support for GloBE Information Return implementation beyond official government and OECD guidance. Key takeaways:

1. **Big 4 Firms** offer free country trackers, tax alerts, and webinars alongside commercial compliance platforms
2. **TEI** provides the most targeted resources for in-house MNE tax professionals, including advocacy on GIR simplification
3. **IFA** offers networking and knowledge-sharing through global congress and branch events
4. **CIOT Pillar Two Award** provides formal professional certification for Pillar Two expertise
5. **IBFD** offers the most comprehensive training programme from fundamentals to advanced technical aspects
6. **Mid-tier firms** (BDO, Grant Thornton, WTS) provide practical implementation trackers and technology partnerships
7. **Tax Foundation** and **Tax Notes** deliver independent policy analysis and research

**Recommendation:** Establish a monitoring routine combining free Big 4 alerts, TEI advocacy updates, and at least one comprehensive research subscription (Bloomberg Tax, Tax Notes, or IBFD) to stay current with rapidly evolving Pillar Two requirements.
