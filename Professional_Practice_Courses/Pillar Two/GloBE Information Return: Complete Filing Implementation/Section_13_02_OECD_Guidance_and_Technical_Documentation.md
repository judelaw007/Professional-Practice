| **Publication Date** | 20 December 2022 |

**Direct Link:** https://www.oecd.org/tax/beps/safe-harbours-and-penalty-relief-global-anti-base-erosion-rules-pillar-two.pdf

**Safe Harbour Types:**

| Safe Harbour | Purpose | Applicable Period |
|--------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Transitional CbCR Safe Harbour** | Deems jurisdictional top-up tax to zero using CbCR data | FY beginning ≤ 31 Dec 2026, ending ≤ 30 June 2028 |
| **QDMTT Safe Harbour** | Deems top-up tax to zero where qualified QDMTT applies | Ongoing (permanent) |
| **Transitional UTPR Safe Harbour** | Relief for UPE jurisdictions with ≥20% nominal rate | FY ending before 31 Dec 2026 |

---

### Transitional CbCR Safe Harbour Detail

**Three Qualifying Tests:**

| Test | Description | Threshold |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **De Minimis Test** | Low revenue and profit jurisdictions | Revenue <€10M AND Profit <€1M |
| **Simplified ETR Test** | Jurisdictional ETR meets transition rate | ETR ≥ Transition Rate |
| **Routine Profits Test** | Profits covered by SBIE | Profit ≤ SBIE amount |

**Transition Rates:**

| Fiscal Year Beginning | Transition Rate |
|----------------------|-----------------|
| 2023 | 15% |
| 2024 | 15% |
| 2025 | 16% |
| 2026 | 17% |

**"Once Out, Always Out" Rule:**
If an MNE Group fails to qualify for the safe harbour in any jurisdiction for any year, that jurisdiction is permanently excluded from safe harbour treatment for subsequent years.

**Data Sources:**
- Qualified CbC Report (primary)
- Qualified Financial Statements (where CbCR not filed)

### Transitional Qualification Mechanism

**Self-Certification Process:**
1. Jurisdiction enacts Pillar Two legislation
2. Jurisdiction self-certifies compliance with GloBE Model Rules
3. OECD publishes on Central Record
4. Full legislative review within 2 years of effective date

**Loss of Qualified Status:**
- Not retrospective
- Applies from date of determination
- MNE groups notified through Central Record updates
