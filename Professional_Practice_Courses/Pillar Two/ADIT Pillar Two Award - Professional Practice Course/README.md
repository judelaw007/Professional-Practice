# ADIT Pillar Two Award - Professional Practice Course

## Course Overview

This Professional Practice Course transforms the ADIT Pillar Two Award syllabus into an implementation-focused training programme for tax professionals working with GloBE/Pillar Two compliance.

**Target Audience:** Tax advisors, in-house tax professionals, and compliance specialists at MNE groups

**Approach:** Implementation-first — teaches HOW to comply, not WHAT Pillar Two is

## Progressive Storyline: Stratos Group

Throughout this course, learners follow **Stratos Group** through its first Pillar Two compliance cycle:

- UK-headquartered technology company
- €950 million consolidated revenue
- Operations in 12 jurisdictions
- Mix of high-tax and low-tax jurisdictions
- Recent acquisition activity (TechStart Ltd)
